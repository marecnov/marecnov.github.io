Scythian plan

16 August 2023

Do not find people with intelligence in a society where the expression of personal opinion is oppressed, in fact, is prohibited.

And what is left to look for people who at least somehow strive for enlightenment, for a high knowledge of new truths. For them, it is a pity to say this, there will be no place in such a public consciousness. It would be more correct to say: its absence.

Quote by Dmitry Pavlovich Runich: "... the Russian peasant ... lives only to satisfy his physical needs and in order to enjoy the freedom that he seeks in vegetable (emphasis added by me - M.I.) life."

The content that I consume that pours from everywhere is a nightmare. There are no people left, only primates, cattle, cotton wool, a herd of cows goes to an empty well for water.

Is it really on this that we, people, will leave our development, really ...

NO, life must be lived Life in order to carry oneself to the Society, to have high manners and to be mentally developed. And for this you can’t stay in a robe ... YOU have rights, you are People ...

- It is useless to shout, they will not hear.

And the last thing: Do not live Death, live with all your courage, consume the Best.
